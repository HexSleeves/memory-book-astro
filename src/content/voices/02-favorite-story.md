You say it now in a way you didn't always. *I'm proud of you.* Just
that. No conditions, no qualifying clause, no *but next time.* Just
the line, often at the end of a phone call, often when I wasn't
expecting it.

I want you to know I save those four words. Every time. They land in
a place that thirty years of work tried and couldn't reach.
