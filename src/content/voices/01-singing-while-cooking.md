You don't realize you do this. We all wait for it. *Great is Thy
Faithfulness* in the kitchen. *How Great Thou Art* on the porch.
Half spoken, half sung, never quite aware that anyone can hear you.

That sound, that low quiet hymn you sing under your breath, is the sound of
my whole childhood. It's the sound that meant the house was steady.
It still does. I hear it now in my own kitchen and I don't even
remember putting it there.
