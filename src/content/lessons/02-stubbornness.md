---
title: How to finish what you start
order: 2
accent: rose
---

You didn't believe in *almost.* You didn't believe in *good enough.*
If we started something, a chore, a paper, a punishment, we
finished it, and we finished it the right way. No half measures. No
sneaking out the back.

Grit, you taught us, isn't a feeling. It's the part where you keep
going after the feeling is gone.
