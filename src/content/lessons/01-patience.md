---
title: That discipline is a kind of love
order: 1
accent: yellow
---

You weren't easy. You woke us up early. You made the bed before the
day made you. You drew lines and you held them, and we hated every
one of them, until we grew up and realized the lines were what was
keeping us standing.

Discipline, I know now, is what you do when you love someone enough
to refuse to let them get away with less.
