---
title: How to be the soft place we land
order: 5
accent: rose
---

Something happened when the grandkids came. The same woman who
wouldn't let a B go, who held us to a standard most adults couldn't
keep, became the brightest, gentlest, most patient soul in any
room. The discipline didn't disappear. It just stopped being the
thing the door opened with.

Now the door opens with a smile. And anyone who walks through it,
me, my kids, anybody, knows that whatever else happens out there,
in here, we are loved completely and we are not alone.
