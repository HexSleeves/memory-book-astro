---
title: That Sunday belongs to something bigger than us
order: 4
accent: sage
---

Sunday morning, no debate. Up, dressed, in the pew before the bells
finished. We grumbled the whole drive over and we sang along anyway.
You taught us that some things are not optional. Faith is a
practice, not a mood, and you don't get to skip it just because the
week was long.

A house held together by prayer is held together. We didn't
understand it at the time. We do now.
