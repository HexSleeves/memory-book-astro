---
title: That a B is not the goal
order: 3
accent: blue
---

A B was a yellow flag. A B meant *we know you can do better.* A B
meant the conversation at the kitchen table, the look, the gentle
unmovable expectation. *You are not the kind of person who settles
for almost.*

We rolled our eyes. We complained. We brought home the As anyway.
Somewhere along the way it stopped being about the report card and
started being about us, and the kind of people you knew we could be.
