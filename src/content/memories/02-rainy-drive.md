Sunday meant *be ready.* The clothes were pressed the night before.
The shoes were already by the door. We could complain, and we did,
loudly and expertly, but we were always in the car on time, because
the car left when you said it would and the church didn't wait.

The hymns I learned from you I still sing under my breath when I'm
nervous. Half the time I don't notice I'm doing it until someone
else does.
