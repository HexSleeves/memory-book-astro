Beds made before breakfast. No exceptions, not even on weekends, not
even when we were sick. *If you can stand up, you can pull up a
sheet.* I used to think the rule was about the bed. I know now the
rule was about the day. It was about not letting it start without
you having already decided to show up for it.

Decades later I still smooth the sheet before I leave the room.
Every time, I hear you.
